What is TypeDB Cosmos?

The "cosmos" is the universe viewed as a complex and orderly system, as opposed to chaos. TypeDB isa database; one that empowers complex systems to be more intelligent through logic, reasoning, and knowledge engineering. The universe of all systems built on TypeDB is the universe of orderly systems -- the TypeDB Cosmos.

By landing on this page, you are either part of the TypeDB Cosmos, or have just found it. In either case, we hope you are excited for what the future holds.
